Seasons

Seasons – a collection of photographs that celebrate the ever-changing seasons of nature and life. In Finland, we experience the beauty of four distinct seasons – spring, summer, autumn, and winter. But just as nature has its cycles, so do our lives, each season representing new moments and experiences.

This collection of photographs features images captured across different timelines and locations, offering a glimpse into the diversity of each season. From the fresh blooms of spring to the quiet stillness of winter, these pictures have been taken in a variety of ways – whether with a phone, a camera, or other lenses – making each shot unique. As you explore, you’ll see how each season, both in nature and life, tells its own story.
